## Color Coding

- **Green**: Payments, positive cash flow
- **Blue**: Stock transfers, documents
- **Orange**: Outstanding amounts, returns, warnings
- **Purple**: Accounting adjustments
- **Gray**: Technical/background entries

## Benefits of This Approach

1. **Immediate Context**: Users see WHY documents are linked
2. **Business Language**: Uses business terms instead of technical jargon
3. **Visual Hierarchy**: Icons and colors make scanning easy
4. **Impact Visibility**: Shows the effect of each linked document
5. **Summary View**: Top-level impact at a glance
6. **Priority Ordering**: Most important entries (payments) shown first

## User Experience Flow

1. User opens a document (e.g., Sales Invoice)
2. Clicks on "Linked Entries" button
3. Panel slides in from the right
4. **Impact Summary** appears at top showing aggregate effects
5. Entries are grouped by type with **business reasons**
6. Each entry shows:
   - Icon representing relationship type
   - Business reason in plain language
   - Impact/effect statement
   - Document name and date
   - Relevant amounts/quantities
7. User can click any entry to navigate to that document
8. Collapsible sections allow focusing on specific types

First-Time Setup & Company Creation

This section will guide you through creating your first company in Versoll Books. You will learn about the welcome page, understand each field in the setup process, and discover how to create a demo company to explore the software.

Welcome Page Overview

When you open Versoll Books for the first time, you will see the Welcome Page. This is the starting point of the software. On this page, you will typically see the following options:

  • Create New Company - For setting up your actual business
  • Create Demo Company - For exploring the software with sample data
  • Existing Companies - If you have already created companies, they will appear here

Choosing the Right Option

If this is your first time using Versoll Books and you want to understand how the software works, we recommend starting with Create Demo Company. If you are ready to use Versoll Books for your actual business, choose Create New Company.

Create New Company

If you want to use Versoll Books for real business accounting, click on Create New Company. This option is for users who want to:

  • Set up their actual business
  • Enter real financial data
  • Use the software for daily accounting operations

When you click Create New Company, you will be taken to the Setup Your Organization page, where you need to enter your company details.

Setup Your Organization Page

The Setup Your Organization page asks for essential information about your business. Each field exists for a specific reason and affects how your accounting data is recorded and reported. Below is a detailed explanation of each field:

Field 1

Company Name

Enter the legal or business name of your organization. This is the official name under which your business operates.

What to Enter

Type the exact name that appears on your business registration documents, invoices, and official records. For example: "ABC Trading Company" or "XYZ Services Pvt Ltd".

Why It Matters

This name will appear on all your invoices, reports, and documents generated by Versoll Books. It ensures consistency in your business communications.

Field 2

Business Type

Select the legal structure of your business from the available options.

Options Available

Typical options include: Proprietorship, Partnership, Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), Private Limited Company, Public Limited Company, and others depending on your country.

Why It Matters

The business type affects how your financial reports are structured and may influence tax calculations and compliance requirements.

Field 3

Industry

Select the industry that best describes your business activities.

Examples

Trading, Manufacturing, Services, Retail, Technology, Healthcare, Construction, and many others.

Why It Matters

This helps Versoll Books optimize default accounting settings based on industry standards and may suggest relevant features for your business type.

Field 4

Country / Region

Select the country where your business operates.

What to Enter

Choose the country from the dropdown list. For Indian businesses, select "India".

Why It Matters

The country setting determines accounting standards, currency, date formats, and tax-related settings applicable to your business. For Indian users, this enables GST-specific features.

Field 5

Financial Year Start Date

Enter the date when your accounting year begins.

What to Enter

For Indian businesses, the financial year typically starts on April 1st. Enter the date in the format: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-04-01).

Why It Matters

All financial reports, profit and loss statements, and balance sheets depend on this date. It defines the period for which your accounts are maintained and reported.

Field 6

Currency

Select the base currency for all your transactions.

What to Enter

Choose from the list of available currencies. For Indian businesses, select "INR" (Indian Rupee).

Why It Matters

This is the default currency for all invoices, bills, payments, and reports. While you can record transactions in other currencies, they will be converted to this base currency for accounting purposes.

Field 7

GST Registration

If your business is registered for GST, you will see fields related to GST.

GSTIN

Enter your 15-digit GST Identification Number. The format is typically: 2 state code digits, 10 PAN characters, 1 entity code, 'Z', and 1 check digit.

Why It Matters

Versoll Books will validate the GSTIN format and enable GST-specific features like GST invoicing, GSTR reports, and automatic tax calculations.

Can I Change These Details Later?

Yes, most of these details can be updated later in the Company Settings. However, we recommend setting up your financial year start date correctly from the beginning, as changing it later can affect your reports and data integrity.

Create Demo Company

If you are not ready to enter real data, you can click Create Demo Company. This option is perfect for first-time users who want to explore the software without any risk.

What is a Demo Company?

A demo company is a sample company that comes pre-loaded with dummy data. This includes:

  • Sample customers and suppliers
  • Example products and services
  • Sample invoices and bills
  • Demonstration of all features

Benefits of a Demo Company

  • Safe Exploration - You can try every feature without affecting real data
  • Learning Environment - Understand the software workflow before implementing it for your business
  • No Risk - Make mistakes freely and learn from them
  • Quick Start - No setup required, start exploring immediately

Creating a Demo Company

To create a demo company:

  • On the Welcome Page, click Create Demo Company
  • Versoll Books will automatically create a sample company with realistic data
  • You can then explore all features, create test transactions, and generate reports

Important Note: The demo company data is for demonstration purposes only. When you are ready to use Versoll Books for your actual business, create a new company and enter your real information.

Summary

You now understand how to create a company in Versoll Books. Whether you choose to set up your actual business or start with a demo company, the process is straightforward. Each field in the setup page is important and affects how your accounting data is managed.

After creating your company, you will be taken to the main dashboard where you can start using all the features of Versoll Books. The next sections of this documentation will explain each part of the software in detail.

Creating Your First Company

Your journey with Versoll Books starts at the Home Page. Unlike other systems that bury setup in deep menus, we've designed a streamlined onboarding wizard right at the front.

1

Access the Home Page

When you first log in, you'll be greeted by the "Create Company" dashboard. If you're a multi-business owner, this is where you'll see all your existing entities at a glance.

2

Basic Information

Enter your Business Name and Legal Entity type (Proprietorship, Partnership, Pvt Ltd, etc.). This information will appear on all your official documents and invoices.

3

Regional & Fiscal Settings

Select your Base Currency (Default: INR) and your Fiscal Year start date. For Indian businesses, this is typically April 1st. You'll also set your Timezone here to ensure accurate transaction timestamps.

4

GST Activation

If your business is GST-registered, toggle the GST option. You'll need to enter your 15-digit GSTIN. Versoll Books will automatically validate the format and prepare your account for GST-compliant invoicing.

Pro Tip: Don't worry if you don't have all your details handy. You can always update your company profile later in the Settings menu, though we recommend setting up GST before creating your first invoice.

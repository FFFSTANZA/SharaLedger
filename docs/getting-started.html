Getting Started

Follow this guide to set up your Versoll Books account and start managing your finances efficiently.

1. Initial Setup

Once you've launched Versoll Books, the first step is to configure your company profile. Go to Settings > Company Profile and fill in the following details:

  • Legal Company Name
  • Business Address
  • GSTIN (if applicable)
  • Base Currency (e.g., INR)
  • Financial Year start month

2. Setting Up Chart of Accounts

Versoll Books comes with a standard Chart of Accounts. You can customize this by going to Accounting > Chart of Accounts. Here you can:

  • Add new expense categories
  • Create bank and cash accounts
  • Manage liability and asset accounts

3. Adding Your First Product/Service

Navigate to Inventory > Items to add the products or services you sell. Make sure to specify:

  • Item Name and Code
  • Selling Price
  • Applicable Tax Rate
  • Initial Stock (for physical products)

4. Creating Your First Invoice

Go to Sales > Invoices and click on "New Invoice". Select a customer (or create a new one), add your items, and click "Save & Submit".

üè¢ Configure Your Business Profile

Navigate to Settings > Company Profile. This is where you define your business identity. Your profile information will appear on all invoices, reports, and official documents.

Essential Fields:

  • Legal Name: Your registered business name (as per government records)
  • Display Name: The name you use for day-to-day operations
  • Address: Your primary place of business (required for tax compliance)
  • GSTIN: Your 15-digit Goods and Services Tax Identification Number
  • PAN: Permanent Account Number (for tax deductions)
  • Email: Primary business email for communications
  • Phone: Contact number for customers and vendors

‚ö†Ô∏è GSTIN Format is Critical! Ensure your GSTIN format is correct (e.g., 27AAAPL1234C1Z5). This 15-character code is used for all GST calculations, E-Way bills, and GSTR reports. An incorrect GSTIN will cause tax filing issues!

üí∞ Initialize Opening Balances

If you're migrating from another accounting system, you need to set your starting point. Navigate to Accounting > Opening Balances to record your current financial position as of your start date.

What to Enter:

  • Bank & Cash Balances: Current balance in each bank account and cash drawer
  • Customer Receivables: Money owed to you by customers (from old invoices)
  • Supplier Payables: Money you owe to suppliers (from old bills)
  • Asset Values: Current value of equipment, inventory, vehicles, etc.
  • Loan Balances: Outstanding principal on business loans
  • Capital/Equity: Your investment in the business

‚öñÔ∏è Balance Check: Total Assets must equal Total Liabilities + Equity. Versoll Books will alert you if your opening balance doesn't balance. This is an accounting fundamental‚Äîif it doesn't balance, you've missed something!

First-time Setup

Ready to get started? Setting up Versoll Books is a breeze. Follow these three simple steps to prepare your business for modern accounting.

1

Configure your Business Profile

Navigate to Settings > Company Profile. This is where you define your business identity. Ensure you provide:

  • Legal Name: Your registered business name.
  • Address: Your primary place of business.
  • GSTIN: Your 15-digit Goods and Services Tax Identification Number.

Note: Make sure your GSTIN format is correct (e.g., 27AAAPL1234C1Z5) as it will be used for all tax calculations and E-Way bills.

2

Set your Regional Preferences

Go to Settings > Localization to set your operational defaults:

  • Base Currency: Choose the primary currency for your books (e.g., INR).
  • Financial Year: Set the start and end dates for your accounting cycle.
  • Timezone: Ensure your transaction timestamps are accurate.
3

Initialize Opening Balances

If you are moving from another software, you'll need to set your starting point. Navigate to Accounting > Opening Balances to record:

  • Current Bank and Cash balances.
  • Outstanding Customer receivables.
  • Outstanding Supplier payables.

Success! Once these steps are complete, you are ready to start recording transactions. Next, let's look at how your accounts are organized.

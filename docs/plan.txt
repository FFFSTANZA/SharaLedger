Versoll Books Documentation Website - Implementation Plan (Tutorial Edition)

Phase 1: Welcome & First Steps (Current)
-------------------------------------------
- The Versoll Books Experience: A high-level introduction to the software.
- Visual Foundation: Establishing a clean, distraction-free tutorial layout.
- Business Profile Setup: Guided tutorial on configuring company details, GSTIN, and base currency.
- Chart of Accounts: Understanding how to organize your finances from day one.

Phase 2: Sales & Customer Success
-----------------------------------
- Customer Directory: How to manage and track your business relationships.
- Invoicing Mastery: Creating, customizing, and sending professional invoices.
- Payment Collection: Recording payments and managing outstanding balances.

Phase 3: Purchases & Vendor Management
--------------------------------------
- Supplier Management: Keeping track of your vendors.
- Expense Tracking: Recording purchase invoices and out-of-pocket expenses.
- Bill Payments: How to record and track payments made to suppliers.

Phase 4: Banking & Reconciliation
---------------------------------
- Bank Integration: Importing statements via CSV.
- Smart Categorization: Using automated patterns to categorize transactions.
- Reconciliation Workflow: Moving transactions from Unreconciled to Reconciled status.
- GL Posting: How bank entries create proper accounting vouchers.

Phase 5: Tax Compliance (GST, TDS & E-Way Bills)
-----------------------------------------------
- GST Readiness: Configuring tax rates and generating GSTR reports.
- TDS simplified: Setting up TDS sections, categories, and handling automated deductions.
- E-Way Bill Management: Generating and tracking E-Way Bills directly from invoices.

Phase 6: Insights & Reporting
-----------------------------
- Reading your Financials: Understanding the Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss.
- Tax Reports: Navigating GST and TDS reports for filing.
- Audit Trails: Using Ledgers to track every transaction.
